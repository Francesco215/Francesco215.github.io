Projects

A collection of some of my projects. Here you will find anything: Small research projects, small SaaS companies with some paying customers, some actual papers, error propagation libraries and more!

Noteician

Automating doctor reports to save them time. We have users and are scaling up.

Probabilistic Q-learning

A way of doing first-principles off-policy RL by treating Q-values as probability distrubuitions that I developed during a weekend

Logvar Diffusion

A weekend project where I developed a diffusion modelling paradigm capable of estimating the confidence of his prediction

Adversarial Takeover of Neural Cellular Automata

A paper in collaboration with Google and Allen Discovery center researchers, showing how to change an organism's properties by altering some of its cells.

Neural Environment

Code to quickly and efficiently train diffusion-based neural environments with just 500 lines of code.

Text-Diffusion

Generates text with diffusion models. A reproduction of the "Continous Diffusion for Categorical Data" paper by Deepmind.

Menzalib

A Python library for processing data from electronic instrumentation, calculating uncertainty, and outputting results to LaTeX.

Quantum Hamming and Reed-Solomon error correction

A repository for the Europe Qiskit Hackathon 2021, focusing on repurposing classic error correction codes for quantum systems.
